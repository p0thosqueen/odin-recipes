Chips and Cheese

Description

A super easy, salty, yummy snack!

Ingredients
  • Shredded Mexican cheese
  • Your favorite tortilla chips
Steps
  1. Lay out chips on plate so that no chips are overlapping
  2. Add cheese to chips
  3. Microwave for 30 seconds or until cheese is melted
  4. Eat by themselves or with salsa!

The easiest upgrade to chips and salsa ever!

Even taught my little sister how to make them!
