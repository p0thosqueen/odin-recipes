Fuzzy Navel Cocktail

Description

A light, citrus and peachy drink

Ingredients
>
  • One part peach schnapps
  • One part Ketel One vodka
  • 4 parts orange juice
  • Sliced orange
  • Full glass of ice
Steps
  1. Add ice to glass
  2. Using a shotglass, pour all parts together
  3. Stir with a sharp knife for the Lingel touch *chef's kiss*
  4. Enjoy responsibly!

This drink is one of my favorite drinks to enjoy when I want something fruity that packs a punch!

Discovered this drink at Christmas Eve with my aunt and it has been my favorite since!
