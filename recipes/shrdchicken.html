Shredded Chicken

A shredded chicken sandwich with poppyseed bun next to chips on wax paper inside a red plastic basket

In a crockpot, combine one large can of boned chicken, one large can (or two small cans) of cream of chicken, one sleeve of saltine crackers, and a dash of poultry seasoning.

Mix this up and set on high for 4 hours or low for 6 hours, stirring occasionally. Serve on hamburger buns with choice of side!
